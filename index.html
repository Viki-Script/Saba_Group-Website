ABOUT SABA GROUP

Pioneers in the Chemical Industry Since 1988

Saba Group of Companies stands as a trusted leader in the field of Speciality Chemicals, Basic Chemicals, and Solvents, with a legacy spanning over three decades. Established in 1988, we have been at the forefront of supplying high-quality chemicals to the Leather, Footwear, and related manufacturing sectors across India and internationally.

With a dedicated workforce of over 600 professionals and a team of technically qualified experts, our strength lies in deep industry expertise, a customer-centric approach, and a wide-reaching commercial network.

Our Network & Infrastructure


Headquartered in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, we operate a centralized warehouse in Perumugai, enabling efficient distribution across India. Our operational reach extends through offices in Chennai, Ranipet, Ambur, Vaniyambadi, Bangalore, Calicut, Kanpur, and Kolkata, allowing us to serve clients promptly and effectively.

Business Divisions


Saba Group is the exclusive India agent for leading global chemical and machinery manufacturers. We deliver high-quality, sustainable, and innovative solutions to the Indian market.
